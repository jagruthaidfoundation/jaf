Our Story

Jagruth Aid Foundation, established in 2023 in Mumbai, is a non-profit charitable trust dedicated to improving lives through education, healthcare, disaster relief, and community development.

The trust supports all citizens regardless of caste, creed, or religion. Our primary goals include providing support to orphans, widows, underprivileged children, the elderly, and physically/mentally challenged individuals. The trust operates exclusively within India and aims to foster unity and social welfare through grassroots action.

Our Board of Trustees

Mr. Kishan Jaiswal

Trustee

Advocate Kishan A.N. Jaiswal is a legal professional with an LLM degree, known for his commitment to social justice and his role as a trustee of the Jagruth Aid Foundation (JAF), a trust formed in 2023 to raise awareness on legal and social issues.

Mr. Chetan Yadav

Secretary / Treasurer

Advocate Chetan Laljee Yadav is a practicing lawyer in the Bombay High Court and various subordinate courts across India. Serving as the Secretary and Treasurer of Jagruth Aid Foundation (JAF), he combines his legal expertise with a passion for social activism. He is dedicated to promoting legal awareness, supporting senior citizens, uplifting underprivileged children, and ensuring that every child in need receives education. With integrity and compassion at the core of his work, he strives to create a society where justice and dignity are accessible to all.

Mr. Aniket Pravin Vaity

Trustee

Aniket Vaity is a passionate social worker devoted to serving society with compassion and purpose. He focuses on supporting underprivileged communities, empowering youth, and promoting education, equality, and dignity for all. Guided by empathy and responsibility, he envisions a more inclusive society and continues to inspire others to join in creating lasting change.
